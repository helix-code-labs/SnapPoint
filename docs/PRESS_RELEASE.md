# SnapPoints Launches Zero-Touch Loyalty Platform for Thailand's Independent Coffee Shops

### Micro-CRM system lets café owners run a full loyalty programme for 199 THB/month — less than the cost of a bag of coffee beans — with no hardware and no app for customers to download

---

**Bangkok, Thailand — March 2, 2026** — SnapPoints today announced the launch of its Zero-Touch loyalty platform, purpose-built for independent coffee shops and micro-businesses across Thailand. The platform lets café owners activate a professional loyalty programme in under five minutes, at a fraction of the cost of traditional point-of-sale loyalty systems.

For most small café owners, keeping customers coming back is their biggest challenge — and their most overlooked opportunity. Traditional loyalty solutions require expensive POS hardware, paper stamp cards that customers lose, or separate mobile apps that most people refuse to download. SnapPoints eliminates all three problems.

Customers collect points by scanning a single QR code printed at the café counter. Because SnapPoints runs inside LINE — the messaging app already installed on virtually every Thai smartphone — there is nothing to download and no account to create. The entire experience takes seconds.

"We are not selling just a loyalty system," said the SnapPoints Founder. "We are selling *continuity* — helping small shops build the kind of customer relationships that big chains take for granted. Our goal is to give micro-businesses the same retention tools as large chains, at a price that makes financial sense from day one."

---

### The Economics Are Simple

At 199 THB per month — Thailand's starting price — a café needs only **13 additional coffee sales per month** to cover the subscription. That is roughly one extra loyal customer returning every two days. For cafés that currently rely on paper stamp cards or nothing at all, the incremental return from even a modest improvement in repeat visits far exceeds the cost.

Merchants who sign up during the launch period receive a **grandfathering guarantee**: the 199 THB/month rate stays locked in forever, with unlimited scans, regardless of future pricing changes for new customers.

---

### How It Works

**For the café owner:**
1. Sign up and receive a unique QR code (printable file provided)
2. Print and place the QR code at the counter — setup is complete
3. Whenever a customer earns a point, the merchant's phone or laptop plays a short audio chime ("ting!") as instant confirmation — no manual action required

**For the customer:**
1. Open LINE and scan the café's QR code
2. Points are credited automatically — balance and progress toward the next reward appear instantly
3. Earned rewards appear in a digital coupon wallet inside LINE; show the coupon to redeem it

---

### Key Features at Launch

- **Frictionless scanning** via LINE LIFF (LINE Front-end Framework) — works inside LINE with no separate app
- **Audio scan confirmation** — the "ting!" sound keeps merchants informed without interrupting their workflow
- **Multi-tier reward system** — merchants configure up to three reward tiers (for example, 5 points for a free upgrade, 10 points for a free drink)
- **Digital coupon wallet** — customers store and redeem rewards directly in LINE
- **Digital shutter** — automatically blocks scans outside configured business hours
- **Fraud protection** — built-in cooldown periods and real-time alerts to the merchant for suspicious activity
- **Merchant dashboard** — daily scan counts, unique customer visits, coupon activity, and scan history

เจ้าของร้านที่สมัครในช่วงเปิดตัวจะได้รับ **การ Grandfathering** — ราคา 199 บาทต่อเดือนพร้อมสแกนไม่จำกัด จะถูกล็อคไว้ตลอดไป ไม่ว่าราคาสำหรับลูกค้าใหม่จะเปลี่ยนแปลงในอนาคตอย่างไรก็ตาม
